\section{de Casteljau's Method}

Consider de Casteljau's method to evaluate a degree \(n\)
polynomial in Bernstein-B\'{e}zier form with control points \(p_j\):
\begin{align*}
    b_j^{(0)} &= p_j \\
    b_j^{(k)} &= (1 - x) b_j^{(k - 1)} + x b_{j + 1}^{(k - 1)} \\
    b(x) &= b_0^{(n)}.
\end{align*}
